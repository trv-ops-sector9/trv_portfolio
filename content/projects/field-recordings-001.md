## About this collection

Started carrying a recorder everywhere in late 2022. No plan, no project — just the habit of capturing things before they disappeared. A subway platform at 11pm. The specific sound a particular crosswalk makes in the rain. The resonance inside a concrete parking structure at 3am when a car drives through one floor above you.

After 18 months I had about 40 hours of material. These 11 tracks are the ones that felt finished — not in the sense of being processed or arranged, but in the sense of being *complete*. Something happened in them.

## The recordings

Most are unedited except for fade-ins and outs. A few are composited from multiple takes at the same location across different days — the Third Avenue bridge recordings, for instance, layer three visits over two weeks. You can hear the weather change.

> Field recording is the practice of noticing. The microphone just proves you were paying attention.

## Technical notes

Recorded on a Zoom H5 with a Telinga parabolic mic for distance work and an internal XY capsule for close environments. No processing beyond normalization and gentle high-pass to remove wind rumble where needed. Mastered at -14 LUFS for streaming.

Available on Bandcamp in lossless FLAC. Pay what you want.
